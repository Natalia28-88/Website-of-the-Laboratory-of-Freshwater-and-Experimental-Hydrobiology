COLLECTIONS

The collections of the Laboratory include the following taxonomic groups:

Group Number of species Number of animal preparations or alcohol fixations
Rotatoria 150 3000
Nematoda 600 7000
Oligochaeta 300 1500
Crustacea 350 3000
Acariformes 300 3000
Tardigrada 80 1000
Insecta 1500 10000

Particularly noteworthy are the materials of the classics of Russian zoology and materials from hard-to-reach areas of the globe.

Crustacea: 2000 preparations of A.L. Bening, V.M. Rylova, S.S. Smirnov and the alcohol collection of tropical zooplankton by S.N. Fernando.

Acarioformes: 300 preparations and fixations of I.I. Sokolov.
